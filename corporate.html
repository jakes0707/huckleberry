Corporate

Corporate events and ticket programs

Another stuffy board room for your next corporate event? No way! You’re more fun and creative than that. Step out of the ordinary and host your next corporate event at Huckleberry's Berry Farm.

We offer a fantastic slate of options for your organization. Need to reward employees or get them excited to reach a new goal? Ask about our employee incentive & perks program. Do your team members need to get in sync with each other toward a common goal? Try our unique group activities and teambuilding events. (Nothing like screaming in unison on one of our thrill rides!) For a quick thank-you, we offer corporate ticket programs so your employees can treat their families to a day at the park for less.

What’s more, we offer completely catered events, so you can leave the details (and the food) to us!

Event Center | Capacity: 300-800

Event Center | Capacity: 300-700
