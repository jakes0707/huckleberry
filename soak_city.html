Soak City

Explore Huckleberry's Soak City

Huckleberry’s Soak City Waterpark is a separately gated, 15-acre water park next to the Huckleberry's Berry Farm theme park. In 2017, Orange County's largest water park expanded to offer even more fun in the sun with seven new slide experiences. Four of the recently added attractions are extreme water slides that let guests climb inside an Aqua-Launch chamber where they wait anxiously for the floor to drop out from underneath their feet. The park now boasts a total of 23 speed, tube and body slides plus the 750,000-gallon Tidal Wave Bay wave pool; the one-third-mile Sunset River; and the three-story Beach House, featuring 200 water guns, nozzles, sprayers and other interactive surprises. The water park also features Gremmie Lagoon, a hands-on water playground for younger kids and families. Open seasonally on select days from mid-May to early September.

Soak City Cabanas - Have it made in the shade!

Have it made in the shade with your very own semi-private cabana during your trip to Soak City. Each cabana reservation comes with up to 8 non-transferrable wristbands and includes extra amenities like a table with umbrella, 4 inner tube rentals, and waiter service. There are three cabanas to choose from: Shore Break Cabanas, Wavepool Cabanas, and Malibu Cabanas. All amenities are the same, just the location of the cabanas is different

Shore Break Cabanas are located near the Shore Break slide structure and the Malibu Cabanas are located near Old Man Falls. See the park map to find where each cabana section is located.

Wavepool Cabanas are located along the sides of Tidal Wave Bay. See the park map to find where this cabana section is located. See the park map to find where each cabana section is located.
